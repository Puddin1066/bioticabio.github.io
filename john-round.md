## Cross-domain execution context

- **[gemflush.com](https://gemflush.com):** An AI discoverability platform that helps businesses get published to public knowledge graphs and then monitors recommendation visibility across assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. Its knowledge-graph publishing model is inspired by open biomedical Wikidata curation patterns (entity completeness, provenance, and update discipline), adapted to commercial entity discoverability and recommendation performance.
- **[placebo-rx.com](https://placebo-rx.com):** A digital wellness concept for chronic pain centered on open-label placebo rituals, multi-sensory engagement, and transparent, AI-assisted self-care feedback. The model is explicitly ethical ("no deception") and explores how expectation, ritual, and interface design can influence perceived symptom relief.
- **Why this is valuable in venture and life sciences:** Together, these projects show an operator lens that spans technical systems, behavior design, and commercialization: building practical products, validating adoption signals, and translating complex mechanisms into user-facing value propositions that can be tested, measured, and iterated.
