# Digital health strategy notes

This page outlines how evidence-led strategy can support digital health teams operating at the intersection of clinical claims, product design, and commercial execution.
{: .lead }

## Core decision problems

- Which clinical claims are supportable now versus later-stage evidence goals?
- How should product strategy align with target or indication-level evidence quality?
- Where can payer/provider conversations be strengthened with reproducible evidence narratives?

## Practical approach

- Keep claim language aligned with evidence strength and provenance.
- Prioritise features and pilots that map to clear clinical decision value.
- Build executive reporting that bridges technical evidence and business risk.
