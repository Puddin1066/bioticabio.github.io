# About

Biotica Bio is a boutique consultancy focused on **evidence-led target and strategy** for life sciences. We help **startups** and **investment firms** move faster with clear, reproducible assessments—so decisions are grounded in the same public evidence that big pharma uses, without the guesswork.
{: .lead }

## Who we are

We use the **Open Targets Platform** (targets, diseases, associations, prioritisation, safety, literature) as our primary evidence base—the same integrated resource that industry and academia use for target validation and drug discovery. Our job is to **interpret** that data and turn it into narratives and recommendations that work for fundraises, due diligence, partner discussions, and portfolio decisions.

## Independence

We do not invest; we only deliver assessments and strategy. That keeps our advice independent and free of conflict.
