# About Biotica Bio

**Why Biotica Bio:** We focus on evidence-led target and indication assessment for life sciences. We help **startups** and **investment firms** move faster by delivering **interpretation and strategy**—not just data—so decisions are grounded in the same public evidence that big pharma uses.

Our assessments are built from a single, reproducible Open Targets query, then interpreted for strategic and due-diligence use. We use the **Open Targets Platform** (targets, diseases, associations, prioritisation, safety, literature) as our primary evidence base and turn it into narratives and recommendations that work for fundraises, due diligence, partner discussions, and portfolio decisions.

We do not invest; we only deliver assessments and strategy. That keeps our advice independent and free of conflict.
