## Intent ownership rules

- Keep one primary intent per page.
- Use internal links to pass users into related pages rather than duplicating the same sales angle on multiple pages.
- Route startup visitors toward `/for-startups.html`, investor visitors toward `/for-investors.html`, and both toward `/contact.html`.
