# Diligence memo framework

This structure is designed for investment committees and executive teams that need concise, auditable scientific decision support.
{: .lead }

## 1) Thesis and decision question

- What exact decision is being made now?
- What must be true biologically and commercially?

## 2) Evidence summary

- Target biology and mechanism.
- Disease association strength and context.
- Druggability/tractability and development constraints.

## 3) Risk register

- Biological risk.
- Translational/clinical execution risk.
- Competitive and positioning risk.
- Adoption and reimbursement pathway risk.

## 4) Recommendation

- Primary recommendation (go, conditional go, hold).
- Conditions and milestones for reassessment.
- 90-day actions and ownership.
